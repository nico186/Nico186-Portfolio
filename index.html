// About

Community is the
product.

I've spent 5+ years building at the frontier of decentralized technology — not from the sidelines, but from the inside. My Web3 journey began in 2020 as an early Keep Network testnet pioneer, and has never stopped since.

From architecting governance frameworks and running global hackathons to shipping 15,000+ branded items internationally and delivering keynotes to 10,000-person audiences — I operate across the full community stack.

I've served as an elected Threshold DAO contributor, community steward at Taho Wallet, and now lead community operations at AcreBTC, the Bitcoin auto compounding protocol earning yield on your assets. My work spans Discord architecture, governance design, IRL event production, content strategy, and AI-assisted community tooling.

I believe the strongest communities are built on trust, cultural fluency, and showing up — in person and online.
